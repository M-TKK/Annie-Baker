I started writing as a teenager to express my most inner thoughts and emotions, unable to share them with those around me. As I retreated into my inner self, I found solace in nature and became aware of an innate energy that flowed through life and connected us all. I hope you can feel some inkling of it resonating through my poems.

As I continued my life’s journey, I gravitated towards a career in holistic and ancient medicines. I am presently a registered Acupuncturist and Chinese Herbalist, and even in my practice today, I still experience those energy flows first-hand.

My first book “Echoes from the Soul” includes some poems I wrote as a teenager. Other poems were written during my travels with exposure to exotic cultures and extraordinary landscapes, my inspiration.

My poem Brethren (page 22) was originally published in the Wildness Society newsletter. Additionally, my short story iPerson earned me an honourable mention in the Atlantis short story competition in the USA.

As I release future poetry, short-stories and novels, I hope you will continue to follow my journey and walk the path with me.
